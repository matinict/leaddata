### 🎬 Classroom Script Structure Tips
Your `classroom_video_renderer.py` expects dialogue tagged as `[T1]`, `[T2]`, `[S1]–[S8]`. Map them like this:
- `T1` → Teacher 1 (explains concept)
- `T2` → Teacher 2 (gives examples/checks understanding)
- `S1–S4` → Students (ask questions, solve steps, react)
- `S5–S8` → Optional peer voices or quiz responders

**Example script snippet (auto-generated by `classroom_script_generator`):**
```
[T1] Teacher1: Today we’re unlocking equivalent fractions!
[S1] Student1: Wait, how can 1/2 and 2/4 be the same?
[T2] Teacher2: Great question! Let’s fold a paper pizza...
[S2] Student2: Oh! Same amount, just cut differently!
```
The renderer will auto-assign colors, emojis, and random animal/shape bubbles per speaker.
